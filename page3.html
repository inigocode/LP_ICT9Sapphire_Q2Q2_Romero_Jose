History of Our Company

Our company was founded on June 15, 1983 by college best friends Steve Workman and Bill Fence from the University of California Berkeley. They founded this company as it was assigned as a final thesis by their teacher Mr. Schwartz. They received a perfect score for this project. After graduating, they decided to move the company headquarters to Manila as it had a booming tech-savvy youth population. Today, they are internationally recognized as one of the 5 unicorn startups from the University of California Bekeley.
